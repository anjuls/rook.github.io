Support

The Rook community is here to support you:

  • Ask questions in our community forums in Slack or GitHub Discussions
  • To request a feature or report a defect, open a GitHub Issue
  • To contribute to the project, see the Contribution Guide
  • For commercial support check out the list of Rook-related support and services companies below.

Commercial Support and Services

Red Hat OpenShift Data Foundation is included with Red Hat OpenShift Platform Plus, a complete set of powerful, optimized tools to secure, protect, and manage your apps.
